Dispatch a ResearchAgent sub-agent to research a question: it searches, reads sources in full, extracts per your extraction_spec, and synthesizes a report — all in an ISOLATED context, so large paper/web content never enters your context. The report is SAVED TO DISK and you get back a one-line conclusion + report path. Use this for ALL literature/paper research instead of fetching papers yourself. After a compaction, re-READ the saved report file (read_file) — do NOT dispatch the same research again. Synchronous: blocks until done (up to 10 minutes).
